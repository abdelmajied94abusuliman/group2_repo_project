Description

Sending a Valentine’s Day card is the perfect way to tell your special someone how you feel. The Quilled Floral Rainbow Heart Card features an ornate floral heart with colorful swirls and multicolor flower petals, the petals are yellow, red, orange, purple, blue, and green.


Each quilled card is beautifully handmade by a highly skilled artisan and takes one hour to create. A quilled card is meant for you to share, treasure as a keepsake, or display as the work of art it is.

Verified Fair Trade Federation Member
